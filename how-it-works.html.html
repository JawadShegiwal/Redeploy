Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the Redeploy process

How long does the matching process take?

Our average matching time is 3-5 business days after receiving complete information about the professionals available. We prioritize speed to minimize career disruption.

Is there any cost for employees?

No, our service is completely free for professionals affected by redundancy. The costs are covered by hiring companies and organizations making redundancies.

How do you ensure data privacy?

We follow strict GDPR compliance protocols. Employee information is only shared with potential employers after obtaining explicit consent from the professional.
